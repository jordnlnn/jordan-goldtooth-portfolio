About Me

My name is Jordan and I enjoy crafting front-end designs that are clean and concise. My interest in web development began in 2008 when I would tinker with MySpace layouts. I didn't realize it back then, but editing and customizing my own layouts taught me a lot about HTML and CSS.

Today, I have the privilege of working at Gold Level Marketing where I build and maintain websites for a variety of clients. While at work, my main goal is to create designs that focus on experiences and funtionality. At home, I'm constantly looking for ways to improve my skill set.
